Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever...

The Problem

The project began with recognizing a common issue: people often experience symptoms and want quick answers...

The Solution

We developed a web app that allows users to enter a list of symptoms they are experiencing...

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms through a user-friendly interface...
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier is trained on a symptom-disease dataset...
  3. Prediction: The model provides likely diagnoses based on symptoms entered...
  4. Additional Info: Description, precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workouts...

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean UI for easy symptom input.
  • Accurate Predictions: Machine learning-based results.
  • Comprehensive Info: Includes full health guidance.
  • Educational Content: Blogs and explanations on health topics.

Conclusion

This web app brings AI-powered health diagnosis to everyone. We hope it increases awareness and empowers users to better manage their health.
